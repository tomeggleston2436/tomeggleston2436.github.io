About me

Hi there, I'm Tom Eggleston, but you can call me "Eggy". I'm a Computing and IT student at the Open University, and as you might have guessed from my site's theme, I like cracking a joke and making fun solutions to problems. After all, who said computing had to be all 0s and 1s?

I'm passionate about exploring the ever-evolving world of technology and having a crack at learning new skills. My areas of interest include web development, Java, andriod app development, but I'm always open to learning something new.

When I'm not studying or working on projects you can find me rock climbing around the local area, paragliding through the sky, or travelling to new places. What do you call an egg who likes to travel? An eggsplorer.
